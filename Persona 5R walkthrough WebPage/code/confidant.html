Jailer Twins - Strength

Strength Confidant has no dialogue, just fusion puzzles, which are:

Rank 1 : Jack Frost with Mabufu

Fuse Silky (Priestess) and Berith (Hierophant).

Rank 2 : Ame-no-Uzume with Frei

Fuse level 16 Suzaku (Sun) with a level 9 Berith (Hierophant).

Rank 3 : Flauros with Tarukaja

Fuse level 9 Berith (Hierophant), level 17 Orobas, and level 16 Eligor with Tarukaja.

Rank 4 : Phoenix with Counter

Fuse level 2 Jack o’ Lantern (Magician) and Hua Po (Hanged Man) to get Yaksini. Fuse Level 20 Yaksini (Empress) and level 9 Kelpie (Strength) to get Phoenix with Counter.

Rank 5: Setanta with Rakukaja

Fuse Silky (Priestess) with Lamia (Empress) to get Setanta with Rakukaja.

Rank 6 : Neko Shogun with Dekaja

Anzu (Hierophant) learns Dekaja at level 28. Then, fuse Anzu (Hierophant), Kodama (Star), and Sudama (Hermit) inherit Dekaja.

Rank 7: Lachesis with Tetraja

Fuse level 29 Principality (Justice) with Tetraja and level 23 Sandman (Magician) to obtain level 36 Thoth (Emperor) with Tetraja. Fuse Thoth with level 25 Anzu (Hierophant)

Rank 8 : Hecatoncheires with Masukunda

Lilim learns Masukunda at level 34. Then, fuse level 34 Lilim (Devil) and level 25 Anzu (Hireophant) while inheriting Masukunda.

Rank 9 : Bugs with Samarecarm

Hariti (Empress) learns Samarecarm at level 41. Then, fuse Hariti (Empress), Pixie (Lovers), and Pisaca (Death) while inheriting Samarecarm.

Rank 10: Seth with High Counter

This can be earned as a skill card from the Mementos rank B mission request, “The Killer who takes out the Trash”. Make Fortuna (Fortune) and Grimehkala (Moon) fuse to make Horus. Then fuse, Isis (Priestess), Thoth (Emperor), Anubis (Judgment), and Horus (Sun) to acquire Seth. Use skill card on Seth after fusion.
